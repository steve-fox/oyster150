How far can you go in London for £1.50? Here are some oyster tips & low cost trips that help the skint, frugal and downright tight to navigate the capital without selling a kidney. Simply by avoiding Zone 1, most * off peak journeys in Zone 2 - 5 will be £1.50 (* yep, that was most).

Oyster 150 is a guide to travelling around London more cheaply. We have tips and gotchas, plus area guides that help you plan trips.
